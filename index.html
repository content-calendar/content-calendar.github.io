🧠 The principles baked into every prompt

  • Hook → Retain → Reward: a headline that earns attention (recent, personal, close-to-home, or surprising), curiosity that holds it (a list, steps, or a story), and a payoff that delivers real value.
  • Give value until they ask: every email hands over something useful on its own — give far more than you ask, so trust builds and they self-select to book.
  • The value equation: frame the outcome they want (a 5 / a confident coder / a college edge), make it feel likely (proof, students like them, low risk), fast (a clear timeline), and low-effort (we do the heavy lifting).
  • The free call is the lead magnet: a complete, valuable diagnostic + personalized roadmap that stands on its own and reveals the natural next step (the program).
  • Clear CTA + a real reason now: one action, plus honest scarcity (capped cohorts, set class times, real deadlines). Be clear, not clever.
